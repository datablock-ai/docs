---
title: "Which countries are excluded from the Spring platform?"
description: "Learn which countries and territories are excluded from using the Spring platform."
---

Users from the following countries/territories are excluded from using the Spring platform:

- Iran
- Myanmar
- North Korea
- Russia
- Crimea, Donetsk, Luhansk, Zaporizhzhia, and Kherson in Ukraine

<Warning>
These countries and territories are highly exposed to sanctions risk, as well as money laundering and terrorist financing risks.
</Warning>
