## Step-by-step process

1. **Create vesting schedule** – Set up your desired schedule; editable via "Edit schedule" button

2. **Prepare distribution** – Select Ethereum network and token to distribute

3. **Generate breakdown** – Choose "Use claiming" option; system auto-updates token amounts. Ensure wallet connectivity to Ethereum chain

4. **Send to claim** – Initiate the claiming process

5. **Approve tokens** – Authorize token spending via on-chain wallet approval

6. **Proceed to payment** – Complete payment by clicking "Pay"

7. **Complete transaction** – Finalize on-chain transaction distributing tokens to Spring's claiming contract

8. **Automatic updates** – Vesting progress updates; investors receive distribution email notification

9. **Token claiming** – Investors log into platform and claim tokens from portfolio deals
