Currently, we have a manual distribution flow that lets you distribute on any chain that is not natively supported by Spring. We are continuously adding distribution support for new blockchains.

## How to Set Up a Non-EVM Distribution with Spring

### 1. Collect Wallet Information
- Navigate to Deal Info > Collect Information
- Gather investors' receiving wallet addresses for non-EVM chains
- Note: investors can change their receiving wallets at a later stage of the fundraise as well

### 2. Prepare Distribution
- Click Prepare Distribution
- Configure blockchain and token address details

### 3. Select Manual Distribution
- View batch breakdown
- Copy cap table
- Choose from recommended distribution services

### 4. Execute Distribution
- Complete distribution through selected service

### 5. Finalize on Platform
- Return to Spring platform
- Leave note documenting manual distribution
- Click Complete Distribution
- Investors will receive an email notification
- The vesting progress will be updated, allowing investors to track the next unlock directly in their portfolios

### 6. Post-Distribution Options
- Enable OTC on deal after completion
