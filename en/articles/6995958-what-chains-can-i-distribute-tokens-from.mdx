---
title: "What chains can I distribute tokens from?"
description: "Learn which blockchains are supported for token distribution on Spring."
---

Spring supports token distribution across 22 blockchain networks:

- Ethereum
- Binance Smart Chain
- Polygon
- Avalanche
- Fantom
- Arbitrum
- Optimism
- Ronin
- Base
- Canto
- Linea
- Blast
- Taiko
- Merlin Chain
- Waterfall Network
- Moonbeam
- Mode
- Sei
- Peaq
- Zetachain
- ZKsync
- Bitfinity

The distribution works with ERC20-compatible tokens.

<Note>
You can also distribute on non-EVM chains through our manual distribution process. See [Distribute tokens on non-EVM chains](/en/articles/7956654-distribute-tokens-on-non-evm-chains) for more details.
</Note>

## Security

The smart contract has been audited and approved by [Certik](https://www.certik.com/projects/presail#audit).
