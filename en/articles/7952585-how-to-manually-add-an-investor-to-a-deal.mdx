## Add an investor to a deal

1. Click on the **Deal** you want to add the investor to.
2. Navigate to the **Fundraising** tab.
3. Click on the blue **Add Investor** button.
4. Add in the information about the investor.
5. Once the investor is added, they will be part of the allocation management.
6. Click on the **Investors Entry** to add any missing information about the investor.
7. If the contact info is missing, the investor can add this in the **Investor Portal Settings**.
8. If the investor is new, the investor must join your platform in the **Investor Portal Settings**. An email will be sent out.
9. When the investor is successfully added, they will be able to view their allocation in their **Portfolio**.
