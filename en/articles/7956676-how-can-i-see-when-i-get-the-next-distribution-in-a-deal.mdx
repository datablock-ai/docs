---
title: "How can I see when I get the next distribution in a deal?"
description: "Check your vesting schedule across all contributed deals and view token distribution timelines through Spring's portfolio interface."
---

The **Portfolio** tab enables you to view the next unlock of all the deals to which you have contributed. Vesting information can be found within a deal in the portfolio section of your portal.

See all opportunities and portfolio data across all platforms you have access to by navigating to the **Global Overview.**

<img src="/images/articles/portfolio-vesting-schedule.png" alt="Portfolio vesting schedule interface" />

<Note>
Spring does not distribute tokens on behalf of any party. The platform cannot provide detailed vesting status information. This information can only be provided by the individual(s) responsible for sending you tokens.
</Note>
