---
title: "Which currencies can you use to raise funds?"
description: "Spring supports raising funds in stablecoins and native coins on a variety of chains."
---

Users can select their preferred blockchain and currency for each fundraising campaign individually. The platform accommodates all EVM-compatible chains and invites users to request additional options not currently listed.

## Supported Currencies by Blockchain

### Ethereum
- ETH (native)
- USDC

### Binance Smart Chain (BSC)
- BNB (native)
- B-USDT
- B-USDC
- BUSD

### Base
- USDC

### Ronin
- RON (native)
- USDC

### Optimism
- USDC
- USDT

### Arbitrum
- USDC
- USDT

### Canto
- Canto (native)
- USDC

### Taiko
- USDC

### Fantom
- FTM (native)

### Polygon
- MATIC (native)
- USDT
- USDC

<Note>
Unlisted chains or currencies can be accommodated upon request. Contact support to add new options.
</Note>
