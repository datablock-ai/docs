## Wallet Tips

**Key recommendations:**
- Ensure browser extensions aren't conflicting with your connection
- Clear browser cookies and cache
- Recommended wallet: **MetaMask**
- Avoid accelerating transactions as this causes problematic behavior

## Investing with a Ledger Hardware Wallet

Setup requires adjusting configurations in both MetaMask and on your Ledger device.

### MetaMask Configuration

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Select **Advanced**
3. Verify **Preferred Ledger Connection Type** is set to "WebHID"

### Ledger Device Setup

1. Connect and unlock your Ledger device
2. Launch the **Ethereum (ETH)** application
3. Navigate to **Settings** using the right button, then confirm with both buttons
4. Enable transaction blind signing when prompted
